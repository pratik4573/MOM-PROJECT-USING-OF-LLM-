 Good morning everyone. The meeting will begin with a silent prayer. Today's meeting is the monthly staff meeting held on June 10, 2022 at 11.30am in the conference hall, Kachin. The meeting is chaired by the principal. All department heads, teaching staff, and administrative staff are present. Two staff members are absent due to medical leave. The principal reviewed the minutes of the previous month and presented a report on academic and co-curricular activities conducted last month. The principal informed the staff about new appointments, updated institutional rules, and decisions taken by the management. Upcoming programs and celebrations for the next month were discussed. The dates for cultural events, academic seminars, and sports activities were announced. The budget allocation for these programs was also explained. Staff members were reminded to follow institutional rules, punctuality, and campus discipline. It was decided that department meetings and staff meetings will be conducted every month. Newly appointed staff members were welcomed and introduced themselves. The principal appreciated staff members who performed well and completed their tasks on time. It was decided to provide support to a staff member who was currently hospitalized. The meeting concluded at 1pm. The next meeting is scheduled for July 30, 2022.
